Project Book Response

Project Book Response is an academic research project which studies the responses of readers to the books they read. We investigate the impact that books have on readers by looking at how they express their reading experiences.

This is a project of the Huygens Institute for the History of the Netherlands (Huygens ING) and the KNAW Humanities Cluster, coordinated by Peter Boot (Huygens ING) and Marijn Koolen (KNAW Humanities Cluster)
